⚡ Omens of the Third Age — Draft Tier List

Grades & analysis by Brute Academy

⚖️ Justifications for grading

  • Offensive power ("zero for 4") & defensive power ("blue block 3" aka bb3)
  • Flexibility — how many things can the card do?
  • Can it make a Lightning Flow? Unconditional? (very important this format)
  • FaB rewards cards that "do a thing" and pitch for resources — we want the most value.
  • Aside: no way to untap your hero in this format.

🧭 Draft strategies (general)

  • 14 cards — 6 class commons, 4 lightning commons, 1 equipment, 2 rares, 1 holofoil.
  • If you see class cards, your opponent is "signalling" they're picking that class.
  • Stay open • Forcing • Wheeling (picks 1–3 come back) • Manipulation • Pivot.

⌨️ Keyboard shortcuts

  • j / k — move down / up   h / l — move left / right
  • Enter / Space — open full-size card   Esc — close / clear
  • / — focus search   gg — jump to top   G — jump to bottom
  • 1–7 — toggle type filters   Q W E — hero filters
